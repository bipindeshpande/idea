## üéØ How to Use Me Effectively

### 1. Be Specific ‚úÖ

**Bad:**
- "Make it better"
- "Fix the thing"
- "Improve the product"

**Good:**
- "Add a 'Next Steps' section to validation reports with 3-5 actionable items"
- "Improve the validation prompt to include specific examples and user context"
- "Add progress indicators showing 'Step 2/5: Analyzing market...'"

**Reality:** The more specific, the better I can help.

---

### 2. Give Context ‚úÖ

**Bad:**
- "Fix the validation"

**Good:**
- "The validation reports are too generic. Users don't know what to do next. Add a 'Next Steps' section at the top with specific, actionable items like 'Contact 10 customers using this email template' with links."

**Reality:** Context helps me understand the problem and solution.

---

### 3. Prioritize ‚úÖ

**Bad:**
- "Do everything"

**Good:**
- "First, add 'Next Steps' to reports. Then, improve the prompts. Then, add progress indicators."

**Reality:** Prioritize what matters most. I can do multiple things, but focus helps.

---

### 4. Test & Iterate ‚úÖ

**Bad:**
- "Build it and forget it"

**Good:**
- "Build it, test it, then improve based on feedback"

**Reality:** Iteration = improvement. Build, test, improve, repeat.

---

### 5. Ask Questions ‚úÖ

**Bad:**
- "Just do it"

**Good:**
- "Can you add X? How would you implement it? What are the trade-offs?"

**Reality:** Questions help me understand what you want and why.
