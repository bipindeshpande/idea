## üí° What We COULD Add

### 1. **Enhanced Intake Questions**
- "What's your biggest concern about starting?"
- "What would success look like to you?" (emotional definition)
- "Have you tried starting something before? What happened?"
- "What's currently holding you back?"
- "How do you feel about risk?" (not just financial, but emotional)

### 2. **Output Enhancements**
- Confidence-building language: "You've got this because..."
- Risk normalization: "These risks are normal and manageable..."
- Decision framework: "If X matters most, pick idea 1. If Y, pick idea 2..."
- Encouragement sections: "You're taking the right steps..."
- Emotional readiness: "You're ready to start when..."

### 3. **Psychological Support Elements**
- Acknowledge fears openly
- Normalize the entrepreneurial journey
- Frame validation as learning, not judgment
- Emphasize progress over perfection
- Celebrate the courage to start
