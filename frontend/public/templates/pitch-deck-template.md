# Pitch Deck Template

**Use this template to create your investor pitch deck. Aim for 10-12 slides maximum.**

---

## Slide 1: Title Slide

**What to include:**
- Company name
- Tagline (one sentence describing what you do)
- Your name and title
- Contact information

**Example:**
```
[Your Company Name]
[Tagline: One sentence that captures your value proposition]

[Your Name], [Your Title]
[Email] | [Phone] | [Website]
```

---

## Slide 2: Problem

**What to include:**
- Clear problem statement
- Who has this problem
- Why it matters
- Current pain points

**Format:**
- Large, bold headline
- 2-3 bullet points
- Visual (photo, chart, or icon)

---

## Slide 3: Solution

**What to include:**
- Your product or service
- How it solves the problem
- Key features
- What makes it unique

**Format:**
- Product screenshot or mockup
- 3-4 key features
- Clear value proposition

---

## Slide 4: Market Opportunity

**What to include:**
- Market size (TAM, SAM, SOM)
- Market growth rate
- Target market segments
- Why now?

**Format:**
- Market size numbers (large and clear)
- Growth chart
- Market segmentation

---

## Slide 5: Business Model

**What to include:**
- How you make money
- Pricing strategy
- Revenue streams
- Unit economics

**Format:**
- Revenue model diagram
- Pricing tiers
- Key metrics

---

## Slide 6: Traction

**What to include:**
- Current metrics (users, revenue, growth)
- Key milestones achieved
- Customer testimonials
- Partnerships

**Format:**
- Growth chart
- Key numbers (large and bold)
- Customer logos or quotes

---

## Slide 7: Competition

**What to include:**
- Competitive landscape
- Your competitive advantages
- Market positioning
- Why you'll win

**Format:**
- Competitive matrix
- Differentiation points
- Market positioning chart

---

## Slide 8: Go-to-Market Strategy

**What to include:**
- Customer acquisition channels
- Marketing strategy
- Sales strategy
- Growth plan

**Format:**
- Channel diagram
- Marketing tactics
- Growth roadmap

---

## Slide 9: Team

**What to include:**
- Key team members
- Relevant experience
- Why this team can execute
- Advisory board (if applicable)

**Format:**
- Team photos
- Key credentials
- Relevant experience highlights

---

## Slide 10: Financial Projections

**What to include:**
- Revenue projections (3-5 years)
- Key assumptions
- Unit economics
- Path to profitability

**Format:**
- Revenue chart
- Key financial metrics
- Assumptions listed

---

## Slide 11: Ask

**What to include:**
- Funding amount needed
- Use of funds
- Milestones you'll achieve
- Expected return

**Format:**
- Funding amount (large and clear)
- Use of funds breakdown
- Timeline and milestones

---

## Slide 12: Contact

**What to include:**
- Company name
- Your name and title
- Email and phone
- Website
- Social media

**Format:**
- Clean, simple design
- All contact information
- QR code (optional)

---

## Design Tips

1. **Keep it simple** - One idea per slide
2. **Use visuals** - Charts, images, icons
3. **Large text** - Easy to read from a distance
4. **Consistent design** - Same fonts, colors, style
5. **Practice** - Know your slides inside and out

---

## Presentation Tips

1. **Tell a story** - Connect slides with narrative
2. **Be passionate** - Show your excitement
3. **Know your numbers** - Be ready for questions
4. **Practice timing** - Aim for 10-15 minutes
5. **End strong** - Clear call to action
