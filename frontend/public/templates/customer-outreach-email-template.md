# Customer Outreach Email Templates

**Use these templates to reach out to potential customers for validation and feedback.**

---

## Template 1: Initial Customer Discovery Email

**Subject:** Quick question about [Problem Area]

**Body:**
```
Hi [Name],

I'm working on a solution for [problem description] and noticed you might experience this challenge.

Would you be open to a quick 10-minute conversation about your experience with [problem area]? I'd love to understand your perspective and see if my solution could help.

No sales pitch - just genuine curiosity about your needs.

Best,
[Your Name]
[Your Title]
[Company Name]
[Email] | [Phone]
```

**When to use:** Initial outreach to potential customers for discovery interviews.

---

## Template 2: Problem Validation Email

**Subject:** Validating a solution for [Problem]

**Body:**
```
Hi [Name],

I'm building [product/service] to help [target audience] solve [problem].

Before I build further, I'd love to validate that this is actually a problem you face. Would you mind answering 3 quick questions?

1. Do you currently experience [problem]?
2. How do you currently handle this?
3. Would a solution that [your solution] be valuable to you?

This will take 2 minutes. Here's a link: [Survey Link]

Thank you!
[Your Name]
```

**When to use:** Validating that the problem exists and your solution is valuable.

## Template 3: Beta Test Invitation

**Subject:** Exclusive beta access to [Product Name]

**Body:**
```
Hi [Name],

I've been working on [product/service] to solve [problem], and I'd love to get your feedback.

I'm offering exclusive beta access to a select group of users. In exchange for your honest feedback, you'll get:
- Free access during beta
- Influence on product direction
- Early adopter benefits

Interested? Sign up here: [Beta Signup Link]

Thanks!
[Your Name]
```

**When to use:** Inviting users to test your product or service.

## Template 4: Follow-Up After Interview

**Subject:** Thank you + Quick follow-up

**Body:**
```
Hi [Name],

Thank you so much for taking the time to speak with me about [topic]. Your insights were incredibly valuable.

Based on our conversation, I wanted to share:
- [Key insight you learned]
- [How you're addressing it]
- [Next steps]

I'd love to keep you updated as we progress. Would that be okay?

Thanks again!
[Your Name]
```

**When to use:** Following up after customer interviews or conversations.

## Template 5: Referral Request

**Subject:** Know anyone who experiences [Problem]?

**Body:**
```

I'm looking to talk to more people who experience [problem] to better understand their needs. Do you know anyone who might be interested in a quick 10-minute conversation?

I'd be happy to return the favor if you ever need introductions in [your area of expertise].

Thanks!
[Your Name]
```

**When to use:** Asking for referrals to expand your customer discovery.

## Best Practices

1. **Personalize** - Always customize for the recipient
2. **Be brief** - Keep emails short and scannable
3. **Clear ask** - Make it clear what you want
4. **Value first** - Offer value, not just ask for help
5. **Follow up** - Don't be afraid to follow up once
6. **Track responses** - Keep a spreadsheet of outreach

---

## Email Sequence Example

**Day 1:** Send initial discovery email
**Day 3:** If no response, send gentle follow-up
**Day 7:** If still no response, send final follow-up
**After:** Move on, but keep them in your database
